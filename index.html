The waste from electronic devices has exponentially increased over the past 5 years. Due to the rapid invitations of recent years and the shorter life span, the electronic waste globally went from 32 Mt in 2010 to 62Mt in 2022, with only 22.3% of e waste being recycled globally meaning in 2022 48.2 Mt of e waste was weather dumped in 3ed world countries, landfills or in the ocean. Leading to the loss of precious earth metals like gold and hazardous substances like lead, mercury, and cadmium. leak into the environment, contaminating soil, water, and air. with experts perdicting e wast will increas to 82Mt by 2030.
